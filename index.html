The Big Lebowski

1998 - Comedy, Crime, Sport
PG-13 - 117 minutes (USA / UK)
IMDB score: 8.1/10
Réalisé par: Joel Coen, Ethan Coen
When "the dude" Lebowski is mistaken for a millionaire Lebowski, two thugs urinate on his rug to coerce him into paying a debt he knows nothing about. While attempting to gain recompense for the ruined rug from his wealthy counterpart, he accepts a one-time job with high pay. After the job is mishandled, he tries to rectify it but is drawn into a comedic, surreal web of kidnappings, ransoms, and bizarre characters...
